About Nusa Heights

A Home Above
the Horizon

Nusa Heights (also known as Nusa Puncak) is a prestigious freehold condominium development nestled in the thriving locality of Gelang Patah, Iskandar Puteri, Johor — one of Malaysia's most rapidly growing investment corridors.


Developed by the reputable SKS Group (MB World), this high-rise residential community comprises 5 blocks with over 910 thoughtfully designed units, offering studio to penthouse configurations in a lush, well-managed environment. Situated with seamless access to the Second Link Highway and Lebuhraya Persisiran Pantai JB–Nusajaya, residents enjoy unparalleled connectivity to Singapore, Johor Bahru city, and beyond.
